Problem Domain Book

Artificial Intelligence in Precision Health by Debmalya Barh (Editor)

This book provides a comprehensive overview of how artificial intelligence is used in the context of precision health — including disease prevention, diagnostics, treatment recommendations, and most importantly for your project — personalized nutrition. It’s an excellent foundation for understanding AI applications in health and wellness domains.

Websites Useful/Related to Your Project

  • PubMed – A comprehensive database of biomedical literature where you can find the latest research on AI, fitness, and personalized nutrition.
  • MyFitnessPal – A popular health and fitness app offering food tracking, nutrition insights, and integration with various wearables for personalized health monitoring.
  • Apple HealthKit – A framework by Apple that enables apps to share health and fitness data, useful for building AI-powered personalized wellness applications on iOS.
