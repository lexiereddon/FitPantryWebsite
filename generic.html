Related Resources

Artificial Intelligence in Precision Health: From Concept to Applications

This book looks at how AI and machine learning are used in precision health with application in personalized nutrition. The chapters look into how the use of AI is tailored to health recommendations, nutrition, and dietary characteristics. This book offers great insights into building AI models for personalized health applications, which is relevant to developing an AI-powered nutrition and fitness assistant.

Machine Learning in Nutrition Research

This article shows the complexity of nutrition data, which makes machine learning a good tool for analysis. Although ML has been used to address issues like obesity and malnutrition many researchers lack expertise in ML which limit the use of broader application. To close the gap the article provides a guide for nutrition researchers on how to use ML in their work. It explains ML fundamentals and distinguishes them from traditional methods. Also gives examples of ML in nutrition research.

Applications of Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Deep Learning in Nutrition: A Systematic Review

This study shows the expanding role of AI in nutrition in the context of Industry 4.0, where automation is important. AI has potential in public health, and the review examines current AI, in ML, and DL in personalized nutrition, dietary assessment, and predictive modeling. Also, it highlights how AI can handle complexity in nutritional data which enables personalized recommendations for improved health outcomes. Lastly, it outlines current challenges and opportunities, providing guidance for future research.
