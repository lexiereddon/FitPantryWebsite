Project Description

FitPantry is a nutrition and fitness application designed to help users make healthy lifestyle choices by tracking and generating custom meal plans and workout routines. Our goal is to simplify nutrition by generating meals that not only conform to a user’s dietary needs but also use the foods a user has available to them. Users can also generate workout routines and track their fitness journey so they can view their progress. The main capabilities of our application revolve around personalizing the fitness and nutrition experience. To accomplish this, users can set their own goals and restrictions such as basic biometric information and allergies as well as their own fitness goals such as losing weight or gaining muscle. Users can also add foods to their pantry that will be used to generate nutritious customized meals that conform to their goals. Workouts are also tailored to everyone by recommending exercises that would help them achieve their goals based on their information. Additionally, the app will allow users to “favorite” the exercises and meals they prefer. This data will be used to create shopping lists so they can continue to cook their favorite meals. FitPantry is currently being developed for use on iOS devices exclusively. We are using Xcode and Swift for the front end, Firebase for the backend and authentication, and PyTorch for AI training in Python.
